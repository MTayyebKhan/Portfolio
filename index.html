About Me

FUN

I think of myself as a fun kind of guy, who doesn't mind what we get upto and can always make an awkward situation more awkward for you, but still funny to me.

OUTGOING

If you were to meet me out and about, you'll realise I like to be the centre of attention in most cases, unless I am hungry, then food is the answer to all worries.

FOOD

Speaking of food, I love food. Like, if you need to eat you should, and don't let anything stop you, more recently though I've been eating abit too much.

GAMER

And what goes well with food? Video games of course. The list of games I have played goes on, but I would be proud to admit World of Warcraft stole my youth.

LEARNER

The habit of gaming over the years has taught me to learn new skills and ways of accomplishing my goals. And will enjoy learning till my brain gives up on it.

TRAINING

Finally, I think we can all agree that we have worked once in our lives, but honestly my favourite training to date has to be MMA and the discipline that comes with it.
